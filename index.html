About Me

I founded a startup a year and a half out of college that allowed me to learn the skills and intricacies of early stage companies. I built software on Xcode. I created demo videos. I mocked up prototypes and wireframes. I pitched to investors, mentors, and customers. I wrote proposals. I convinced co-founders to join me. I managed off-shore talent. I built financial models, business model canvases, and customer archetypes. I pounded the pavement. I read everything there is to read about starting companies. I failed. I learned.

I believe products should be useful, simple, and beautiful.

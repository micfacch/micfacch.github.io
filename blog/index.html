MeetingSpace: Video Conferencing Design

Written by Michael Facchinello on August 12, 2012.

Dec 2013 - Creation of a new product for BigMarker.com, a video conferencing company. Project completed with a 4-person multidisciplinary team. Deliverables included user personas, wireframes, clickable prototype, site map, branding, and market positioning strategy.

MeetingSpace does not seek to simply make meetings virtual, it seeks to fix the problems of physical meetings in a virtual space.

Splash Page

Frictionless meeting hosting

Simple Agenda creation, editing and sharing

In meeting notes and collaboration

In meeting task assignment that seamlessly integrates into calendar adds accountability

Simple interface for meeting management

Post meeting summary including agenda, notes, and action items, plus meeting statistics like participation

Tools like go to meeting cost a lot of money, and fail to fix the fundamental productivity problems faced in meetings. MeetinSpace was designed to change that.
